Are you tired of thinking what are you going to cook for your family during the week? Worry no more! Mestre Cuca combines recipes and creates a menu according to your directions. Say you want to eat fish, pork and chicken this week and you also want some salad. Mestre Cuca takes those directions, searches in a list of recipes (that you have entered yourself or your friends have shared) and suggests a lunch and dinner distribution for the week, filling in the blanks with other easy to cook and healthy suggestions. You don't have to think about which dish you will prepare on Monday, which one on Tuesday, etc. Mestre Cuca does that for you. Oh, and the more you use Mestre Cuca the smarter she gets. She will not repeat the same dish every Monday, or every Wednesday (unless, you know, you tell her to!)

Have I told you that Mestre Cuca creates the grocery list for you? Well, yes; she does! And if you take the time to tell Mestre Cuca what you already have in your pantry, she can recommend you a few dishes without the need for you to go to the supermarket! Cooking should be fun. Use Mestre Cuca and see for yourself. OK, she will not cook for you... Sorry :-)

But that is the mean side of the Cuca!

FAQ

What is the meaning of the site name?
"Mestre Cuca" are the words in Portuguese for "Master Cook", or Chef. But it is a word play with the folkloric character of the Cuca, a witch that haunts children (uuh... scary!). Mestre Cuca will use its magic to prepare menus for you!
What will Mestre Cuca do for me?
Mestre Cuca will use a collection of recipes and prepare a menu for the house wife to use during the week. Mestre Cuca will use its "magic" in order to prepare a list of dishes that are non-repetitive, easy do cook and healthy! You can also tell Mestre Cuca what type of dishes you want to have and she will create a super custommized menu for you. You can use your own recipes or you can use your friends' recommendations.
